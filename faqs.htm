Is this Operating System is Open source?

Sure! Our distrobution is fully open sourced and free do not need to pay licens to use it this Project is under GPL(Gnu Public Licens) you can find our distrobution on GitHub

What this Distro Aims With others distros?

It is an Egyptian distribution and will have a strong reputation among other distributions. It will focus on user comfort and ease and will attract all people, regardless of their computer experience, and will be useful in any place or field.

what is new in this distrobution that is others do not have?

We want to offer more than one special thing, the first of which is We offer a stable distribution based on Arch, so you can always get the latest packages without worrying about stability. The latest packages always give you a better experience in terms of support, whether for the hardware or the optimization of the existing software, because the PC is a huge world and full of different parts. Secondly, we will be working on the Obsidian Apps Kit, which will include tools that will facilitate the user experience, whether for the new user who is looking for simplicity and ease, or for the professional who is looking for accuracy, because all the tools will be available as gui apps and cli apps.

What About Security?

Let us assume that the problem occurred in the (xz) package recently, so to increase stability, we will freeze the package from the Arch repositories, and we will be responsible for updating it after examining it and ensuring its stability and security.

What about how team manging the distro?

Distributions don't just include installers! The idea is that management and decisions have a big and huge factor, and the quality of the user experience has a bigger factor. This is the first example of Ubuntu, which has the worst management and many users abandoned it.
